About me

In January 2023, I successfully completed my doctoral studies in machine learning with a focus on healthcare applications at KU Leuven. Passionate about machine learning and its transformative potential in healthcare, I specialize in leveraging data-driven approaches to drive impactful insights and improve patient outcomes. With a focus on applying advanced algorithms to complex medical datasets, I strive to develop innovative solutions that bridge the gap between cutting-edge technology and patient care. Through my research, I aim to contribute to the development of intelligent systems that can revolutionize healthcare delivery and empower medical professionals to make informed decisions. Let's connect and explore how we can drive positive change together.
